Privacy Policy for Zapwick

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Zapwick ("the app," "we," "us") is a remote control for LG webOS smart TVs. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle. We built Zapwick to respect you, and that includes your privacy.

The short version

Zapwick does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not sell your personal information. The app talks to your TV over your own local Wi-Fi network. The only data leaving your device goes to Google, to show ads (unless you purchase the ad-free upgrade) and to process that purchase.

Information stored on your device

To connect to your TV, Zapwick stores the following locally on your phone only. This information is not transmitted to us and we never see it:

  • Your TV's network address (IP), name, and — if you enter it — its MAC address, used to connect and to power the TV on.
  • A pairing key issued by your TV during setup, so you don't have to re-pair each time.
  • Your preferences, such as pinned favorite apps and saved TVs.

You can clear this at any time using "Forget this TV" in the app, or by clearing the app's data in your phone settings.

Permissions the app requests, and why

  • Nearby devices / Location: Android requires the nearby-devices permission (and, on older versions, location permission) to scan your local Wi-Fi network for your TV. Zapwick uses this only to discover your TV. The app does not collect, store, or share your geographic location.
  • Microphone (only if you use voice input): When you tap the microphone button on the keyboard, Android's built-in speech recognizer converts your speech to text. This is handled by your device/Google's speech service; Zapwick does not record or store audio.

Advertising

The free version of Zapwick shows ads through Google AdMob. To provide ads, Google may collect and process certain information, including your device's advertising ID and general device information. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn about and control ad personalization in your device settings under Google → Ads.

Users in the European Economic Area and the UK are shown a consent choice before personalized ads are used, as required by law.

If you purchase the ad-free upgrade, ads are removed.

Purchases

The optional one-time "ad-free" upgrade is processed by Google Play Billing. Zapwick does not receive or store your payment details; Google handles the transaction. Google may share a record of the purchase so the app can confirm your upgrade.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account information.
  • We do not track your viewing habits or what you watch.
  • We do not sell personal information to anyone.

Children's privacy

Zapwick is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page.
